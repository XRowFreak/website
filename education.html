Education

The academic journey and intellectual development of José Rizal

Academic Pursuits

Rizal's pursuit of knowledge across multiple disciplines and countries

Early Education

  • Taught by his mother, Teodora Alonso, at an early age
  • Private tutors in Calamba, Laguna
  • Binan School under Maestro Justiniano Aquino Cruz

Ateneo Municipal de Manila (1872-1877)

  • Bachelor of Arts degree
  • Graduated with highest honors (sobresaliente)
  • Studied Spanish, Latin, Greek, and philosophy

University of Santo Tomas (1877-1882)

  • Studied Philosophy and Letters
  • Transferred to Medical School
  • Left without completing degree due to discrimination

Studies in Europe

  • Universidad Central de Madrid - Medicine and Philosophy
  • University of Paris - Additional medical studies
  • University of Heidelberg - Ophthalmology specialization

Linguistic Achievements

Rizal mastered 22 languages including:

Spanish French German English Italian Dutch Japanese Arabic Latin Greek
